Rainbow Tables

Instead of calculating the whole password list, calculate one password, take a reduced version of
the hash and calculate a hash of the hash
Doing this multiple times creates a chain of hashes
Store the first and last of the chain – if a hash you are trying to find is the last value of a chain, then the plaintext must be somewhere along the chain (probably)
