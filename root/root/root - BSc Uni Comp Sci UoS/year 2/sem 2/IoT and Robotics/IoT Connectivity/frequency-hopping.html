rapidly switch between several pre-defined channels within the band

eg : 

the 2.4 GHz band is divided into 79 (Bluetooth Classic) or 40 (Bluetooth Low
Energy)

 

Hopping Sequence: Both devices agree on a synchronized "hopping sequence," a predetermined order in which they jump between channels

 

Short Packets: Data is transmitted in small packets, further reducing the chance of
interference within any single channel.

 

Adaptive Hopping: This means devices can dynamically avoid channels experiencing interference
