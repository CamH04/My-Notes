UART

Universal Asynchronous Receive Transmit:

  • Serial, Async
  • 2 lines , one recive one send
  • The receiver must lock onto the data and detect individual bits
  • starts with a starting bit, usually by driving
    logic low for one clock cycle. In the next n clock cycles, n bits are sent
    sequentially from the transmitter until a stop bit is sent
  • Char is 8 bits usualy
  • Can have a parity bit:
    • Parity can be set to "odd" or "even," and it ensures that the total number of bits set to
      logic "1" in a character is either even or odd
