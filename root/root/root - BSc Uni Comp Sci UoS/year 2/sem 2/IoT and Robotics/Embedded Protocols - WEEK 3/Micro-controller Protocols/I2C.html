I2C

I 2 C (Inter-Integrated Circuit):

  • 2 wire , half duplex , synchronous communication
  • 2 signal lines (a clock and signal)
    • SDA (Serial Data Line):
      •  (1 byte) Bidirectional line for transmitting and receiving
        data. 
      • Both Controller and Peripheral devices can
        transmit data on this line.
    • SCL (Serial Clock Line): 
      • Carries the clock signal that synchronizes data
        transfer
      • • The master device generates the clock signal
      • • Slave devices use the clock signal to know when
        to read and write data.
  • allows for multiple devices to be connected to 1 bus
  • share a single pair of data and clock lines
  • used for Micro-controller to peripheral communication 
  • the I2C bus is used for integrated chips on a PCB 
  • a single I2 C bus supports up to 127 devices
  • Low power consumption
  • Noise interference is common
  • speed is limited
